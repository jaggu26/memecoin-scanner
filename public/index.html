Section 2: How to Read the Token Cards
Understanding the Score & Signals
🎯 SCORE (0–10)
• 0–4 = Too weak, skip
• 5–6 = Entry signal (yellow, moderate conviction)
• 7–8 = Strong entry (purple, high conviction)
• 9–10 = VERY STRONG (green, rare)

🛡️ SAFETY BADGE
• ✅ SAFE — Passed all security checks (liquidity, no honeypot)
• ❌ RISKY — Honeypot detected or suspicious holder distribution
• ⚠️ UNVERIFIED — Could not verify, check manually

📊 KEY METRICS
• 5m/1h: Price % change (green = up, red = down)
• Liq: Liquidity ($) — higher is safer
• MC: Market cap — sweet spot is $200K–$10M
• Vol: Trading volume in 5 min
• B/S: Buys vs Sells — more buys = bullish
Section 2a: Breakout Score (B:0–10) — How It's Calculated
What Makes a Token Score High?
+3 pts — Huge Volume Spike
5-minute volume ≥ 5% of the full 24h volume (extreme buying activity right now)

+2 pts — Volume Spike
5-minute volume ≥ 3% of 24h volume (significant buying surge)

+3 pts — Strong Price Pump
Price up +15%+ in the last 5 minutes (very strong momentum)

+2 pts — Solid Price Move
Price up +8–14% in 5 minutes

+2 pts — Safe Liquidity
Liquidity ≥ $100K (less rug risk, easier to exit)

+2 pts — Market Cap Sweet Spot
MC between $200K–$10M (enough room to 10x, not already mooned)

+2 pts — Strong Buy Pressure
≥100 transactions in 5m with 65%+ buys vs sells

+1 pt — 1h Momentum Confirmed
Price also up +20%+ over the last 1 hour

+1 pt — Fresh Token
Token is 2–48 hours old (early opportunity)

−2 pts — Honeypot Warning
Zero sells in 5m while buys exist (can't sell = honeypot risk)

−2 pts — Dangerous Price Drop
Price fell −10%+ in 5 minutes (dump in progress)

−2 pts — Very Low Liquidity
Liquidity under $10K (extreme rug risk)
Section 2b: Technical Score (T:0–12) — How It's Calculated
Strategy 2 — Trend + RSI + Volume + ATR
+2 pts — Uptrend (EMA Proxy)
1h price change is positive AND 24h is not down more than 5%. Means short-term and medium-term trend both up.

+2 pts — RSI in Buy Zone (55–75)
Approximated from 1h momentum: RSI ≈ 50 + (1h change × 1.5). Sweet spot means momentum without being overbought.

+3 pts — Volume Surge
1h volume is 2× the hourly average (24h vol ÷ 24). Strong institutional or whale buying.

+1 pt — Volume Above Average
1h volume beats hourly average but not by 2×. Moderate interest.

+3 pts — Full Breakout
24h price is up +5%+ AND 1h is still positive. Confirmed uptrend with continuation.

+1 pt — Soft Breakout
6h price up +3%+ AND 1h positive. Early breakout forming.

+2 pts — High Volatility (ATR)
5-minute absolute move > 5%. High ATR means strong momentum — good for quick trades.

+1 pt — Moderate Volatility
5-minute move is 2–5%. Some momentum present.

💡 When to use which score?
Use Breakout Score for short momentum plays (5min–4h holds).
Use Technical Score for trend-following trades (1h–7day holds).
Best entries: B ≥ 6 AND T ≥ 7 — both scores agree = higher confidence.
Section 2c: Paper Trades Tab — How to Use It
Test the Strategy Without Real Money
What is Paper Trading?
Paper trading = pretend trading with virtual money. You click "Buy" at today's real price, and the scanner tracks your P&L live. No real crypto is bought — it's purely for testing whether our breakout signals work before you risk real money.

Step 1 — Open a Position
On the Live Scanner tab, find a token you want to track. Click the green 📈 Buy $100 (Paper) button at the bottom of the card. It records that token's exact current price as your entry.

Step 2 — Watch the Paper Trades Tab
Go to 📈 Paper Trades. You'll see:
• Entry Price — what price you "bought" at
• Current Price — live price (auto-updates every 30s via the scanner)
• P&L % — your profit or loss percentage
• P&L $ — dollar gain/loss on the virtual $100
• Time Held — how long since you opened the position

Step 3 — Close a Position
When you want to "sell", click the red Close button. The trade moves to Trade History with the final P&L recorded.

Step 4 — Analyse Your Stats
The summary row shows your overall:
• Total P&L — sum of all closed trade profits/losses
• Win Rate — % of trades that were profitable
• Closed Trades — total number of completed paper trades

💡 Strategy Validation Tips
• Paper trade for 1–2 weeks before going live.
• Only buy when B ≥ 6 AND T ≥ 7 — wait for both scores to agree.
• If your win rate stays above 50% after 20+ trades, the strategy is working.
• Trades persist after page refresh — your history is saved in your browser.
Section 2d: Accuracy & Smart Filter — Real-World Testing Results
How Accurate Is MemeScan? (~70% With the Smart Filter)
Overall Accuracy
Raw scanner output (all B≥5 tokens) hits around 50% accuracy — half the signals are genuine momentum, half are noise. Applying the Smart Filter below pushes accuracy to ~70%.

🎯 The Smart Filter — 4 Rules That Improve Accuracy to ~70%

Rule 1: Breakout Score ≥ 6 (not just ≥ 5)
The jump from 5 → 6 filters out weak or marginal signals. Only trade confirmed breakouts.

Rule 2: Liquidity > $30,000
Tokens with <$30K liquidity are one big wallet away from a rugpull. This single rule eliminated most false positives in our testing.

Rule 3: 1-Hour Change is Positive (1h > 0%)
If the token is dumping on the 1h chart, the 5m spike is likely a dead-cat bounce. Only enter when 1h trend confirms momentum.

Rule 4: More Buys Than Sells in 5m
Buys > Sells on the 5m window confirms real buying pressure. If sells dominate, it's distribution — not accumulation.
What the 5 Scoring Fixes Eliminated
The scanner was updated with 5 automatic penalty rules that filter out known false positive patterns:
• No-activity tokens — 0 buys AND 0 sells in 5m → automatically scores 0
• 1h dump penalty — if 1h drop >15%, score is reduced by 2 points
• Sell-dominant penalty — if >65% of transactions are sells, score −1
• Single-whale trades — large volume from 1–2 transactions no longer triggers volume spike signal
• Near-zero liquidity — <$5K liquidity now gives −3 penalty (was −1)

What to Expect After Filtering
From a typical scan of 200 tokens, applying the Smart Filter will leave you with 5–15 genuine candidates. Of those, roughly 70% will sustain price momentum over the next 30–60 minutes. The other 30% still lose — memecoins are inherently volatile. Always use stop-losses.

⚠️ Even 70% accuracy means 3 in 10 trades lose. Never risk more than you can afford to lose on any single trade. Use paper trading to validate before putting real money in.
Section 3: The 5-Point Scam Filter (Use This!)
Before You Buy ANY Memecoin
1. Liquidity > $30K?
If liquidity is less than $30K, the coin can be rugpulled easily. Skip it. 🛑

2. Liquidity Locked?
Check if LP tokens are locked in a locker contract (not in owner's wallet). This prevents rugpull.

3. No Wallet Holds > 20%?
If one person owns 50%+ of tokens, they can dump and crash price. Red flag! 🚨

4. Sells Happening Normally?
If ONLY buys are happening (0 sells), it's likely a HONEYPOT — you can buy but can't sell! Avoid! 🍯

5. Chart Not a Straight Pump?
Tokens that pump 1000% in 1 minute = pump & dump scheme. Don't buy at the peak. Wait for pullback. 📉

⚠️ IF ANY ANSWER = NO → SKIP THAT COIN

Section 5: Glossary (Simple Definitions)
Breakout: Token that suddenly spikes in volume + price (buying pressure)
Liquidity: Money locked in the trading pool. Higher = easier to buy/sell
Market Cap: Total value of all tokens in circulation. Shows how "big" the coin is
Honeypot: Scam token where you can BUY but CAN'T SELL. You're trapped!
Rug Pull: Developers steal liquidity + disappear. You lose 100%
Volume: Number of tokens bought/sold in a time period. High = lots of traders
Slippage: Difference between expected price & actual price. High slippage = lose money
LP Lock: Liquidity locked in a smart contract, preventing theft. Good security feature
Smart Money: Experienced traders. If they hold <20%, it's safer to trade
Pump & Dump: Insiders pump price, then dump all tokens & crash it. You lose
