How This Tool Works
Breakout Scoring System (0–10)
Every 30 seconds, we scan 100+ tokens from DexScreener. Each gets a score 0-10 based on these signals:
Signal 1: Volume Spike (3 pts max)
5m volume ≥ 5% of 24h = 3 pts (🔥 huge spike)
5m volume ≥ 3% of 24h = 2 pts
5m volume ≥ 1.5% of 24h = 1 pt

Signal 2: Price Momentum (3 pts max)
+15% in 5 min = 3 pts (very strong)
+8% in 5 min = 2 pts
+3% in 5 min = 1 pt
-10% in 5 min = -2 pts (avoid!)
Signal 3: Liquidity Safety (2 pts)
≥ $100,000 = 2 pts (safe to trade)
≥ $30,000 = 1 pt
< $10,000 = -2 pts (rug pull risk!)

Signal 4: Market Cap (2 pts)
$200K–$10M = 2 pts (10x potential)
$50K–$200K = 1 pt (early, risky)

Signal 5: Buy Pressure (2 pts)
Buys > Sells by 65%+ = 2 pts
Trade Strategy (Sangeeta's Recommendation)
Stop Loss + Take Profit Rules
⛔ Stop Loss: -15% — Exit immediately if price drops 15% from entry
🎯 Take Profit 1: +50% — Sell 50% of position (lock in profit)
🎯 Take Profit 2: +100% — Sell remaining 50% (double your money)
⏱️ Max Hold: 7 days — Exit if no movement in 7 days

Score 5+ = Enter (Breakout signal)
Score 7+ = Strong entry (high conviction)
Score below 5 = Skip
⚠️ Critical Warnings
🔴 Rug Pulls: Always check liquidity lock before buying. If liquidity < $30K, skip.
🔴 Slippage: Memecoins have high slippage. Set 5-10% slippage on DEX.
🔴 Position Size: Never put more than 5-10% of capital in one memecoin.
🔴 FOMO: If score is high but you missed the 5m candle, wait for next signal.
🟡 Network: Use Solana for cheapest fees ($0.001/trade vs $5-50 on Ethereum).
